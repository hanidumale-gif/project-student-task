Frequently Asked Questions

Is StudentTracker really free?

Yes! Our basic version is completely free forever with no hidden charges. You can manage up to 50 tasks, set reminders, and access all core features without paying anything.

Can I use it on my phone?

Absolutely! StudentTracker is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile browsers. We also offer native mobile apps for iOS and Android with Pro and Premium plans.

How do you protect my data?

We use industry-standard encryption for all data transmission and storage. Your academic information is private and secure, and we never share your data with third parties.

Can I export my data?

Yes, all plans allow you to export your tasks and progress data in multiple formats (CSV, PDF) for backup or use in other applications.
